**Features**

* Easy-to-use interface with a numeric keypad.

* Calculates final product price including fixed shipping cost of 850 BDT per kg.

* Allows profit margin estimation for your business.

* Clear and concise display of costs for quick decision-making.

**Usage**

* Enter the product price in the calculator.

* Enter the product weight in kg. The calculator automatically adds 850 BDT per kg for shipping.

* Click if you want to add inside bangladesh shipping or not. 

* Press numbers/operators on the keypad to calculate total cost.
